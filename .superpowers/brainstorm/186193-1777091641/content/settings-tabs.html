A — Top Tab Bar

Horizontal tabs across the top with an underline indicator. Tab/Shift+Tab cycles between tabs. Familiar pattern, compact, leaves maximum space for content.

B — Side Tab Panel

Vertical tab list on the left, content on the right. Left/Right arrows switch tabs, Up/Down navigate entries. More room for tab labels, feels like a proper settings screen.

C — Segmented + Number Keys

Pill-style segmented control with number key shortcuts (1–4 jump directly to tab). Fast keyboard navigation, very compact. Norton Commander-ish feel.

💡 All options will also support a "Search settings" shortcut (like / to filter) for quick access without tab navigation.
